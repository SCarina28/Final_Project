Mission

As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeolders - To protect and promoye the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based and complete basic education.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to build the nation.
